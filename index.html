Brand guidelines · v1.0

The quiet confidence of a wellness platform.

Kaivros sits at the intersection of digital calm and physical presence. Lilla is the soul, cream the breath, orange the pulse. This is the single source of truth for every mark, color, word and pixel we ship.

01 — Manifesto

What Kaivros stands for

Our brand lives in the pause before a session, in the warmth of a cream surface, in the clarity of a single sentence. Think: a spa that publishes API docs.

Calm

Soft, never sterile. Warm cream surfaces replace pure white. Transitions are slow (0.2s), never snappy.

Confident

Professional without pretense. We say what things do, in sentence case, without exclamation marks.

Bilingual

English-forward with Italian heritage. "Corpo · Testo · Leggibilità" sits comfortably next to "Brand Identity".

Rules

Do

Pair the logo with a matching background variant. Always leave clear space equal to the cap-height of the K on all sides.

Do

Use the primary lilla logo on cream and white. Use the cream variant on lilla-lt surfaces.

Don't

Never recolor the wordmark. Never apply drop shadows, gradients, outlines or effects to the logo.

Don't

Don't place the logo on busy photography, low-contrast backgrounds, or inside containers smaller than 64px tall.

07 — Voice & tone

How Kaivros sounds

Bilingual, casual-professional, second-person. Wellness-adjacent without being woo-woo. Specific over clever.

Sentence case, always

Headings and buttons feel like speech — not marketing shouting.

"Start session" — not "START SESSION"

Second person

Warm, direct, addressed to one reader. "You" and "tu" freely.

"Your sessions, arranged."

Short, declarative

Verbs over nouns. Specific over abstract. Trust the reader.

"Start your session." — not "Session initialization."

No emoji, no hype

Type and color carry the emotional tone. No exclamation marks, no "Let's go!".

"Ready when you are."

Middle-dot rhythm

Use · to pair concepts: "Corpo · Testo · Leggibilità".

"Logo · Titoli accent"

Bilingual, unapologetically

Italian and English coexist. Don't translate "Kaivros". Let warmth carry across languages.

"Your sessions · Le tue sedute"
